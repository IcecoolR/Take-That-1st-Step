
Mental Health:
-> Give yourself a compliment daily?
-> Let yourself relax for 30 minutes?
-> Do a breathing exercise for 3 minutes?
-> Write a reflective journal daily?
-> Practice a new skill for 20 minutes?
-> Choose a different route to or from work or school today?


Physical Health:
-> Do some lighter exercise: Yoga, Tai Chi, Stretching?
-> Do a more demanding activity: Swimming, Cycling, Walking?
-> Take the stairs not the lift?
-> Consume less sugar?
-> Cook something new and healthy?
-> Having a set bedtime and getting up time?


Social Health:
-> Random acts of kindness?
-> Actually call a friend or family member?
-> Use technology to stay in touch with people?
-> Play a game?
-> Take 5 minutes to find out how someone is really feel?
