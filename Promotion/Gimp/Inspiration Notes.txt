Mental Health:
-> Give yourself a compliment daily?
-> Let yourself relax for 30 minutes?
-> Do a breathing exercise for 3 minutes?
-> Write a reflective journal daily?
-> Practice a new skill for 20 minutes?
-> Choose a different route to or from work or school today?


Physical Health:
-> Do some lighter exercise: Yoga, Tai Chi?
-> Do a demanding activity: Swimming, Cycling, Walking?
-> Try to walking more for a week?
-> Consume less sugar for 14 days?
-> Cook something new and healthy for 31 days?
-> Stick to a sleeping scheduled?


Social Health:
-> Do a random act of kindness?
-> Call a friend or family member?
-> Confess a regret to a loved one?
-> Play a game?
-> Take 5 minutes to find out how someone is really feeling?
-> Speak to someone new for 3 days?
